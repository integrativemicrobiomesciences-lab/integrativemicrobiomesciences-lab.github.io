Dodge is an Australia Awards Scholar currently pursuing a Master of Genome Analytics at Monash University. 
He joins the Integrative Microbiome Sciences Lab with more than a decade of experience in public health research, serving as a Science Research Specialist at the National Tuberculosis Reference Laboratory of the Research Institute for Tropical Medicine (RITM; Philippines).

His primary research focuses on the application of next-generation sequencing to combat infectious diseases. 
Specifically, Dodge investigates the phylogenetic and functional diversity, transmission dynamics, and drug resistance patterns of *Mycobacterium tuberculosis* as it shifts in time and space. 
As a member of the Asia PGI TB Genomics Expert Working Group, he aims to generate high-impact genomic data that informs policy decisions and improves public health outcomes in the fight against tuberculosis.
